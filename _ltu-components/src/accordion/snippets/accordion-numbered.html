How to install and run the VPN

Windows

  1. Click Start and expand the Cisco folder in the applications list.
  2. Click Cisco AnyConnect Secure Mobility Client.
  3. Enter ltuvpn.latrobe.edu.au in the Ready to connect field and click Connect.
  4. Enter your La Trobe username
    • Staff should enter their username as JBloggs
    • Students should enter their username as STUDENTS\01234567
  5. Enter your La Trobe account password and click OK.
  6. Click Accept.

Mac OS

  1. In Finder, click Go and select Applications.
  2. Double-click the Cisco folder.
  3. Double-click Cisco AnyConnect Secure Mobility Client.
  4. Enter ltuvpn.latrobe.edu.au in the Ready to connect field and click Connect.
  5. Enter your La Trobe username:
    • Staff should enter their username as JBloggs
    • Students should enter their username as STUDENTS\01234567
  6. Enter your La Trobe account password and click OK.
  7. Click Accept.

For mobile devices, see our guides for Android and iOS(iPhone or iPad).
