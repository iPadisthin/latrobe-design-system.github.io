Human, transferrable skills

Build the human skills that transfer to any context. Communication. Innovation. Creative thinking. You’ll develop your ability to listen, empathise, think critically and problem solve.

Take the next step

Do what you love

Explore what interests you. Anthropology. English. History. Philosophy. Sociology. Politics. Languages. But also topics you might not expect. Environmental humanities. Sustainability and development. Visual cultures.

Keep it broad or narrow down

Choose from a huge range of general and specialist degrees, majors and minors. Combine topics that wouldn’t usually go together, like politics and statistics or anthropology and neuroscience. Keep your options open and graduate with a broad set of skills – or specialise.

The different people around you

You’ll be part of a linguistically, culturally and ethically diverse cohort. Together, you’ll challenge your thinking and tackle real-world problems through debate and discussion in intimate classrooms.
